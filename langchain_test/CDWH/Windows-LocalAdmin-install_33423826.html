Approach using Antragsformular

  1. Open "antrag" in browser
  2. Fill in your personal information. Gütlig bis: tick "unbefristet"; Funktion: "andere" and "IT"
  3. Upon completing 2), you can choose what you want to apply for, choose (and type your machine ID into "PC Inventar-Nr." :
  4. Click "Antrag abschicken"
  5. Bram will receive a message he has to sign off first, before this is granted

Using \administrator user (likely depreciated)

  1. Open the Local Computer Management:
  2. You are asked for credentials that have administrative permissions, use another account:
  3. Get the user/password from KeePass:
  4. Browse to System -> Lokale Benutzer und Gruppen -> Gruppen, right click on group Administratoren and choose Zur Gruppe hinzufügen...:
  5. Click the button Hinzufügen...:
  6. Enter your user lead by MS\ and click Namen überprüfen:
  7. You're asked for credentials again, choose Anderes Konto verwenden:
  8. Enter your personal credentials this time and click OK:
  9. Your name should have been resoved now, then click OK:
  10. You should see your user in the list of group members now, click OK to save the changes:
  11. Close the Local Computer Management and then Reboot your VD
