DataWarehouse : Create a bill

Created by Michael Hadorn, last modified by Fabian Franzeck on Jan. 05, 2023

Scope

These are instructions for creating a bill/invoice in order to charge e.g. a research project (or any other party) for services provided by our team. This is a very simple process: You fill in an online form that asks you the relevant questions regarding VAT/MWST. in order to automatically create an appropriate invoice. You'll need to upload some sort of "contract" with the person to be billed: For internal issues, emails are an appropriate form. The billing department will immediately create the bill and put the money on our account. They will followup with the projects, check for paying delays, etc. - it's a carefree process for us!


Instructions:

  1. Contact Zeiser Patricia patricia.zeiser@usb.ch (Honorar- und Drittmittelbuchhaltung) to create a user for the Rechnungsstellungsplattform
  2. go to: https://ictsapgralp01.uhbs.ch/inf2/forms.php   (Fabian: for me this only works from Internet Explorer, not Chrome or Firefox)
  3. Choose: Rechnungsstellung intern/extern

  4. Fill in Fondsnummer FO140230 for our account (Forschungskoordination), click "Schritt 2"

  5. Ansprechpartner: Should be by default "Herr Dr. Stieltjes Bram", click "Schritt 3"

  6. Choose appropriate options (typically 30 days Zahlungsfrist), click "Schritt 4"

  7. Rechnungsempfänger: For Projects at USB, you have to enter "207373" (USB interne Weiterverrechnung), external projects have to be judged on an individual basis. Click "Schritt 5"

  8. Indicate Bill title. For USB projects, write the name of the person to be billed in the "Betreff" with a "c/o". The choice of type of "Leistung" decides if e.g. VAT/MWST. has to be paid: Typically choose "Interne Weiterverrechnung", then click "Schritt 6"

  9. Indicate amount: "Preis" is the total price, "Menge" is typically 1 "Pauschale". Write Details in the "Positionstext". Click "Schritt 7"

  10. Upload Contract: For USB projects, email prints with approval and indication of price are enough, no formal contract needed. Click "Eingaben prüfen"

  11. Click "Absenden"

  12. Success!
