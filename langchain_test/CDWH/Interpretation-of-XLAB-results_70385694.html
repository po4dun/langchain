DataWarehouse : Interpretation of XLAB results

Created by Fabian Franzeck, last modified on Nov. 16, 2022

The results in XLAB are a string, that can contain a big variety of results: numeric ones, qualitative ones, invalid ones, etc. In IL_FCT_LABOR_RESULT_XLAB we already generate LRX_RESULT_VALUE_NUMERIC within the DWH which parses numeric values out of the string.

The interpretation of results is summarised here, however this is only a incomplete suggestion to start with (however covering most of the frequently asked labcodes) and the lab continously adds new ones.

SELECT
    r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE AS LRX_RESULT_VALUE_ORIG,
    r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE_NUMERIC , 
    r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE_COMPARATOR,
    CASE
        WHEN lower(r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE) IN (
            'negativ' , '+' , '++' , '+++' , '++++' , '-' , '+-', '-+', '+/-', 'neg.'
        ) 
        OR lower(r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE) LIKE '%pos%'
        OR lower(r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE) LIKE '%neg%' 
        OR lower(r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE) = 'mässig'
        THEN r. LRX_RESULT_VALUE
        WHEN r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE = '4+' THEN '++++'
        WHEN r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE = '3+' THEN '+++'
        WHEN r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE = '2+' THEN '++'
        WHEN r.LRX_RESULT_VALUE IN ('1+', '(+)') THEN '+'
        ELSE NULL
    END AS LRX_RESULT_VALUE_qualitative
    ,
    CASE
