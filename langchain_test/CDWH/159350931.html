Application Information

Administrative core application for admission-, medical service registration-, billing- and master data management of patients. Please check the following links for more information:

  • Application Short-Description USB: ISH Patientendatenverwaltung

  • SAP Consent Administration Manual: see Chapter 8

In May 2022, large areas of ISH have been migrated to the new S4/HANA ERP system. Just Patient- and Case-Management remains in the ISH-Sidecar, as S4/HANA doesn’t cover the healthcare industry solution. Logistics and Finance are now part of the S4/HANA-System (i.e. Material Masterdata and Organisation-Structures).
