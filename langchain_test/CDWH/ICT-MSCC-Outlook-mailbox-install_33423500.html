DataWarehouse : ICT-MSCC Outlook mailbox install

Created by Fabian Franzeck, last modified on Juni 13, 2022


The Ferienkalender of the team and technical Mails from Dataservices Jobs are sent to a central mailbox: ict-mscc@usb.ch


Request Access


  • Open a SOS-Ticket and ask for access to ict-mscc@usb.ch for your user

Configure Mailbox

  • Open Control Panel:
  • Open User Accounts:
  • Open E-Mail Configuration:
  • Click button "E-Mail-Accounts...":
  • Choose your account from the list and click "Change":
  • Open Advanced Settings:
  • Open "Erweitert" tab and click "Add":
  • Enter "ict-mscc" in the text box and click OK:
  • When your access rights have been granted, the mailbox appears in the list, then click OK:
  • Click Next:
  • Click Finish:
  • Close the dialogue:
